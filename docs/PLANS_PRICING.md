# QReady – Plans & pricing

Three tiers: **Free**, **Business**, **Pro**. Mid tier = “for business” (unlimited queue + branding). Top tier = “full control” (your own promos in the ad slot).

---

## Tiers at a glance

| Tier       | Price   | Queue limit | Logo & colours | Ads on customer queue screens        |
|-----------|---------|-------------|----------------|--------------------------------------|
| **Free**  | £0/mo   | 5 people max | No             | Third-party ads (with promo links)   |
| **Business** | £10/mo | Unlimited   | Yes            | Third-party ads (with promo links)   |
| **Pro**   | £20/mo  | Unlimited   | Yes            | **Your own** promos (with links)     |

---

## Free

- **£0 per month**
- Up to **5 people** in the queue at a time
- No logo or colour customisation
- Third-party ads shown on the customer’s handset (queue screens), with links to the promo

*Good for: trying QReady, very low volume.*

---

## Business

- **£10 per month**
- **Unlimited** people in the queue
- **Customisation:** your logo and colours
- Third-party ads shown on the customer’s handset (queue screens), with links to the promo

*Good for: real day-to-day use, looking like your brand, no queue cap.*

---

## Pro

- **£20 per month**
- **Unlimited** people in the queue
- **Customisation:** your logo and colours
- **Your ads:** you use the ad space for your own promotions (image + copy + link). No third-party ads in that slot.

*Good for: full control, promoting your own offers, app, or next visit.*

---

## Optional later

- **Annual billing:** e.g. Business £100/year, Pro £200/year (roughly 1–2 months free).
- **Grandfathering:** if prices go up, existing subscribers keep current price for a period.
